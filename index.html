The NITK English Medium School (formerly called as KREC English Medium High School) was established in the year 1968 with the objective of meeting the needs of an English Medium School for the children of the employees in the NITK Campus who hail from different linguistic parts of the country. The school is open to the members of the public also.
Since then the school has developed by leaps and bounds. The school adopted the CBSE syllabus in 2008-09 and is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi.
NITK English Medium School provides liberal, national and essentially Indian Education conductive to inculcate the the pupils a spirit of patriotism, democratic outlook, a sense of duty and discipline. Education imparted in the school generally prepares the students to play the role of well disciplined citizen of our great nation with an all round developed personality.
